Market Pulse

A personal investing tool that tells you when to buy, when to hold, and when the market is overheated — using live NIFTY data and a momentum signal called RSI.

"Be fearful when others are greedy, and greedy when others are fearful." — Warren Buffett

One screen. Three decisions.

No spreadsheets. No guessing. Just a clear signal based on where the market momentum actually is.

The Signal — RSI Zones
RSI goes from 0 to 100. Here's what it means.

RSI (Relative Strength Index) measures momentum — how fast and how far prices are moving. It doesn't predict the future but it tells you when the market is stretched too far in either direction.

<30
BUY Zone
Market beaten down. History says this is where long-term wealth is made.
30–70
HOLD Zone
Normal range. Keep existing positions. No new lump-sum buys.
70–80
CAUTION
Market extended. Pause buying. Optionally sell a call option for income.
>80
HEDGE
Overheated. Stop all new buys. Income strategy — sell calls aggressively.
Two Timeframes — Double Confirmation
We check the signal twice before acting.

One RSI alone can give false signals. Using two timeframes together filters out noise and gives higher confidence before recommending an action.

2-Hour RSI

Short-term momentum

NIFTY price data is fetched every 1 hour from Yahoo Finance. Two 1-hour bars are combined into one 2-hour bar. RSI is then computed on these 2H bars.

Purpose: Catch intraday/short-term extremes — when market has moved too fast in a few sessions.

Daily RSI

Trend confirmation

Separately, the daily closing prices of NIFTY are fetched. RSI is computed on the full daily chart — one bar per trading day.

Purpose: Confirm the bigger trend. A 2H spike alone is noise. If Daily RSI also agrees, the signal is real.

How It's Connected
Data flows in one direction — from market to you.

Nothing is stored on a server. Your portfolio stays in your browser. The app only reads data — it never writes or transacts anything.

Fetches raw candles, resamples 1H → 2H, computes RSI for both timeframes, applies zone thresholds, and generates the BUY / HOLD / CAUTION / SELL verdict. Runs fresh on every Refresh.

Your Screen

ETF Monitor UI

Shows the Market Pulse signal, both RSI gauges, your portfolio P&L, each ETF's individual RSI, the deploy sizing guide, and the hedge calculator — all in one view.
